                Use of the mobile live demonstration unit for educational purposes. Through
                this our experts provide informative sessions on understanding not only the

Social Responsibility

                WFarmland Rainwater Harvesting Systems has setup its Research and Manufacturing 
                Plant in areaof 5acres At Hadihalli Village MalalurGram Panchayath limits Chikmagalur
                District-known for its Coffee Fragrance.Using advanced and modern technology, the
                plant had been conserving rain water drop by drop from the last three years and has
                played a pivotal role in improving the groundwater source. Our Company has never 
                forgotten its social responsibility at any point of time. Whenever any calamities 
                occurred in country the Company feels proud to say that has fulfilled its responsibility 
                with great interest. It is known that COVID-19 pandemic wreaked havoc in 2020 
                and affected all sectors. During this period Farmland Rainwater Harvesting  Systems, 
                realizing its responsibility, responded positively to problems faced by the people.At 
                that time the Company distributed food kits to them. The journalists too struggled hard 
                during this critical period to keep  pandemic under control.They were also given food kits 
                to help their families.
